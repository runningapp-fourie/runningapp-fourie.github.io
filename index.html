Two Intuitive Modes for Every Run

Split Mode

Automatically split your run by distance or time. Set up 1 km, 1 mile, or custom intervals, and Fourie handles the timing and announcements effortlessly—perfect for structured interval training, tempo runs, or race pacing.

Lap Mode

Take full manual control. Simply start your run and tap to mark laps or sets whenever you're ready. No pre-setup required. Add a minimum pace threshold to ignore pauses or walking breaks, ensuring only true running effort counts toward your laps.

Personalized Features to Keep You Motivated

Custom Audio Cues

Tailor voice feedback for pace, distance, time, splits, and more—choose your preferred order and frequency to stay informed without glancing at your screen.

Customizable Views

Arrange your run screen exactly how you like it. Display the metrics that matter most to you for quick, at-a-glance insights during your workout.

Whether you're training for a marathon, hitting intervals on the track, or enjoying a casual jog, Fourie delivers accurate GPS tracking, detailed stats, and seamless performance—all in a clean, runner-focused design.
