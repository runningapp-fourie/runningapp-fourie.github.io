Fourie

Elevate your running experience with Fourie, a powerful and intuitive app designed specifically for runners who demand precision and flexibility in tracking all your runs.

Two Intuitive Modes for Every Run

Split Mode

Automatically split your run by distance or time. Set up 1 km, 1 mile, or custom intervals, and Fourie handles the timing and announcements effortlessly.

Lap Mode

Take full manual control. Simply start your run and tap to mark laps or sets whenever you're ready. No pre-setup required. Lower your wrist to activate Rest Mode and skip adding lap count when you start your lap, so that your laps will be numbered correctly both during and after your run.

Personalized Features to Keep You Motivated

Custom Audio Cues

Tailor audio cues for pace, distance, time, splits, and more. You can choose your preferred order and frequency to stay informed without glancing at your screen.

Customizable Views

Arrange your run screen exactly how you like it. Customize buttons and metrics that matter most to you for quick actions and at-a-glance insights during your workout.

Detailed Insights

VO2 Max

Optionally display your estimated VO2 max after every run to track improvements in cardiovascular fitness and endurance.

Lap-by-Lap Breakdown

Dive into detailed metrics for each lap, including pace, distance, and time, for precise analysis of your splits.

Heart Rate Zone Analysis

Visualize time spent in each heart rate zone to optimize training intensity and recovery.

Seamless Control on Your Wrist

Wrist Flick Gesture

Enjoy seamless one-handed control during your workouts with intuitive wrist flick gestures. Customize them to effortlessly start or end runs, mark laps etc. without even lookcing at the screen.

Wrist Down Gesture

For interval training, simply lower your wrist for a customizable duration to enter Rest Mode. Starting new laps won't advance the overall lap count, letting you track actual intervals without pre-planning.

Action Button Support

Take full advantage of your Apple Watch Ultra's Action Button. Customize it to quickly start or end runs or mark laps right from your wrist.

Built by runners, for runners.

Whether you're training for a marathon, hitting intervals on the track, or enjoying a casual jog, Fourie delivers accurate GPS tracking, detailed stats, and seamless performance in a clean, runner-focused design.
